The plot of Elden Ring takes place in Inter-Earth, a country ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal.
In the past, the life of the Inter-Earth depended on the Elden Ring, from which the World Tree grows.
They both endowed the lands with “golden light” - a special kind of grace sent to all the inhabitants of this land

Some features of the game:


Open World. Contains various hazards and areas to explore, including multi-level castles with intricate layouts.
_
Movement. The playable character can move around the open world either on foot or riding a horned horse named Potok.
_
MBoss battles. There are a total of 238 bosses in the game, although you only need to defeat 12 of them to progress through the game
